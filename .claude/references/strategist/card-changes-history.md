# 2026-05-22
[Effective Strike](https://www.vekn.net/forum/rules-questions/83009-clarification-effective-strike#117087)

Ineffective strikes still affect the striking minion, and can provide press and/or maneuver.

[Malkavian POD](https://www.blackchantry.com/2026/01/19/84-vampire-the-eternal-struggle-cards-added-to-print-on-demand/)

- Fabrizia Contreraz: She gets -2 votes also outside referendums.

# 2025-06-12
[Sabbat V5 Release](https://www.blackchantry.com/2025/06/12/new-sabbat-rules-terms-and-errata/)

New rule: **Sabbat Paths**.

New terms: **Stun:** and **Queue a combat**.

- Cardinal Benediction: Now prevents non-Sabbat vampires from casting votes and ballots.
- Mind Numb: Now stuns the minion.
- Private Audience: Now costs no blood.
- Rutor’s Hand: Now enters play locked and requires to lock the card to unlock.
- Wall Street Night, Financial Newspaper: The card is now locked when the minion attempts the action to move counters.

- Tabriz Assembly: The blood gain is now optional.
