# Modules catalog — DRAFT for owner review

A module is a group of cards played together for a synergy reason. Decks are read as
compositions of modules: identify them first, then judge densities and fit (see
`/strategy/deck-building` for the density classes). A module's value is context-dependent —
the same package can be payload in one deck and tech in another.

Provenance: mined 2026-07 from the site's archetype pages and strategy articles, cross-checked
against TWDA co-occurrence (lift-based mining, 1,584 decks 2019+). `[N decks]` = decks since 2019
containing the core of the module. Entries flagged ⚠ need owner confirmation.

Combat is deliberately ONE section among many: it gets a dedicated article on the site because
its module space is wide, but it is not the default focus of deck analysis — payload, delivery,
defense and economy come first (except when analyzing an actual combat deck).

## Bloat & pool economy

- **Leech package** — Villein, Blood Doll, Vessel, Minion Tap (+ hunting grounds, Jake Washington,
  The Coven, Life in the City, Perfectionist to refill). The universal bloat: pool from minions'
  blood, then regenerate the minions. Villein/Vessel are trifles; Minion Tap has no 5-cap limit —
  the right leech for cap 9+ crypts.
- **Zillah + Villein ramp-bloat** — Zillah's Valley, Villein, Information Highway, Dreams of the
  Sphinx. Big caps out 1-2 turns early, then most of their blood back as pool: acceleration whose
  net pool cost approaches zero. The backbone of big-cap politics and powerbleed. [Zillah+Info
  Highway also merge with Metro Underground in MMPA lists]
- **Tap → Voter Cap pump** — Minion Tap, Voter Captivation (superior), Dmitra Ilyanova / Honor the
  Elders-style refills. Tap each big vampire to 2-3 blood, refill via passed referendums, tap
  again. At 7-8× Voter Cap this is bloat heavy enough to be the deck's whole defense (all-forward
  politics, cf. calibration.md case A). [22 decks: Voter Cap + Perfect Paragon + Awe + Minion Tap]
- **Vote bloat** — Consanguineous Boon (breed decks), Parity Shift (Princes/Justicars), Ancient
  Influence + Political Stranglehold (big caps), Benefit Performance, Amici Noctis. Pool gain at
  referendum scale; virtually every political deck carries some.
- **MMPA economy** — The Parthenon, Ashur Tablets, Liquidation (+ Cybele, Nana Buruku,
  Huitzilopochtli, Rumors of Gehenna for extra master phase actions). Extra MPAs turn master-card
  bloat/recursion into an engine; Liquidation stocks the ash heap Ashur feeds on. [22 decks:
  Liquidation + Unleash Hell's Fury + Golconda — the Girls build]
- **Anarch economy** — Carfax Abbey, Papillon, The Anarch Free Press, Twilight Camp, Garibaldi-
  Meucci Museum, Powerbase: Los Angeles. Hunt-boost + regeneration + recursion, the blood engine
  behind Anarch toolboxes. [49 decks: Carfax + Garibaldi + Monkey Wrench + Revolutionary Council]
- **Sacrifice bloat** — Golconda: Inner Peace, Khobar Towers, Redeem the Lost Soul (+ Soul Gem /
  Antediluvian Awakening tricks). Convert a spent or endangered vampire into pool; also the exit
  valve of star engines (Ghede, Cybele).
- **Fast-game bloat** ⚠ — Momentum's Edge, Gambit Accepted. Pays only if the first oust comes
  early; a speed-deck signature more than a module.

## Acceleration & crypt ramp

- **Big-cap ramp** — Information Highway, Zillah's Valley, Dreams of the Sphinx (+ Monastery of
  Shadows as vote-era supplement). 4 transfers become 6-8 effective; the standard way to field a
  cap 10-11 star by turn 2-3. Count copies to infer the plan: 5×+5× aims at 3-4 big vampires, not 2.
- **Govern ramp** — Govern the Unaligned (superior), then inferior as bleed once the board is set.
  The single most efficient dual-purpose card in the game: acceleration early, payload late.
  Common to weenie/midcap DOM, Lasombra, Ventrue, Giovanni builds. [107 decks: Govern +
  Conditioning core]
- **Breed kits** — The Embrace, Web of Knives Recruit, Childe of the Revolution, Third Tradition:
  Progeny (+ clan enablers: Palla Grande, Foundation Exhibit, Conductor, The Eldest Are Kholo,
  Legacy of Pander). Swarm A&B and vote fodder; pairs with Consanguineous Boon ("breed & boon")
  and exposes the deck to Scourge of the Enochians.
- **Crypt-cycling** — Wider View, Anarch Convert, Effective Management, Coroner's Contact. Dig the
  crypt for the star or feed crypt-dependent engines (Little Mountain Cemetery).
- **Merge assembly** — the natural merge is slow and draw-dependent: control one version, the other
  in the uncontrolled region, spend 4 transfers + 1 pool (advanced goes on base; base card text
  still applies afterward, advanced supplies capacity/disciplines/sect and wins conflicts). Legacy
  (played by an unmerged *advanced* vampire — +2 stealth action — fetches the *base* from crypt /
  uncontrolled / ash heap and merges on the spot) and Epiphany (the mirror: an unmerged *base*
  vampire fetches the *advanced*) turn a single off-copy into a tutor target — so the crypt runs one
  version as the workhorse and a lone copy of the other purely as the fetch target, no random draw
  needed. The reason to bother is needing BOTH texts (an advanced ability *and* a base ability that
  survives the merge), not just the stronger single side; Legacy is the tool when the advanced is the
  workhorse (fetch the rare base), Epiphany when the base is. Wider View / Effective Management /
  Anarch Convert's crypt-draw dig toward the off-copy more slowly, without spending an action.

## Card flow & recursion

- **Cycling suite** — Dreams of the Sphinx, The Barrens, Elder Library, Heart of Nizchetus,
  Fragment of the Book of Nod, Powerbase: Los Angeles. Anti-jam for decks with a strong situational
  module (stealth, intercept); 1× Barrens next to Dreams signals a deliberately tight module.
  Heart of Nizchetus ("Heart of Cheating") is the toolbox default.
- **Ashur engine** — Ashur Tablets (+ The Parthenon for MPAs, + Liquidation to stock the heap,
  + Rumors of Gehenna). Recursion *and* bloat: reshape the deck against the table mid-game. Post-
  2024 errata: check dates on older lists.
- **Giovanni recursion** — Sudario Refraction (2× fetches each other back), Spiritual Guidance.
  Constant access to the two cards the lunge needs.
- **Fetch locations** — Garibaldi-Meucci Museum (Anarch; also contested to deny its S:CE to
  others), The Sargon Fragment (open to all, action-hungry), Maabara, Unity.
- **Magic of the Smith toolbox** — Magic of the Smith fetching Heart of Nizchetus first, then the
  situational answer (Bowl of Convergence vs stealth, Sniper Rifle vs Potence, Ivory Bow, Kevlar
  Vest, Soul Gem for star engines). Equipment as a searchable answer suite. [24 decks: Magic of
  the Smith + Rutor's Hand]
- **Alastor fetch** (Justicar/Inner Circle) — Alastor searching out Helicopter (unlock after a
  successful action: the voter also bleeds, cf. the Lutz Helicopter variant) or a big gun at half
  cost (Assault Rifle, AK-47 — prohibitive at full price, a bargain fetched). A +1 stealth
  political action that ignores requirements: equipment tech for titled crypts, teeth for vote
  decks. Seen in Lutz Politics, Mistress, Banu Politics.

## Bleed payload & delivery

- **DOM bleed core** — Govern the Unaligned, Conditioning, Bonding, Foreshadowing Destruction,
  Threats (+ Seduction as the in-discipline block denial). The strongest raw bleed; Foreshadowing
  and Daring the Dawn are lunge cards, kept not cycled. [107 decks]
- **DEM bleed kit** — Kindred Spirits, Eyes of Chaos, Confusion (+ Deny, Touch of Clarity).
  Bleed with built-in bloat and stealth support; the weenie DEM signature. [29 decks]
- **Permanent bleed enhancers** — Camera Phone, Club Illusion, Enkil Cog, New Carthage (titled
  Brujah), Legendary Vampire, Reg Driscoll, Tasha Morgan + J.S. Simmons. Board-based bleed growth:
  survives hand jam, compounds with swarms; the reason "weak bodies remain" doesn't stop a lunge.
- **Unlimited-modifier stacking** — Leverage, Command of the Beast, Quicksilver Contemplation,
  Power of One [pot]. Bleed bonuses that don't count against the one-limited-modifier rule: stack
  past the normal ceiling, and obscure the true bleed power in hand.
- **Off-turn bleed** — Enkil Cog, Madness Network: the actual engines — they grant the
  out-of-turn action. Bleed during the prey's turn (dodges Archon Investigation — it's out of
  turn) or the predator's (wins the Edge); doubles a star's output. Needs the vampire unlocked,
  so it pairs with unlockers (Homunculus, Metro Underground, Mylan Horseed) — necessary pieces,
  not engines. Signature of The Unnamed, Rachel Madness, Thucimia, Blind Spot.
- **Bounce-proof payloads** — Shroud of Decay (superior), Line Brawl [cel] pool steal, Anarch
  Revolt / Smiling Jack / Constant Revolution / Dragonbound / Tension in the Ranks damage
  permanents. Pool damage that no bounce or reduction answers; the standard second payload of
  walls and rush decks (Monkey Wrench as the lunge on top).
- **Swarm-of-ones** — many 1-bleed minions (weenies, Legionnaires, Servitors, embraces). Beats
  bounce economically: bouncing a 1-bleed is a losing trade, and Archon Investigation never fires.

## Stealth & block denial

- **OBF stealth suite** — Lost in Crowds, Faceless Night, Swallowed by the Night, Spying Mission
  (+ Cloak the Gathering / Veil the Legions to cover allies and swarms). The reference stealth
  module. [141 decks]
- **OBF block denial** — Elder Impersonation (+ Hide the Mind to counter AUS bounces). The
  full-purpose denial; forces walls to overcommit intercept before it reveals. [34 decks]
- **Shadow suites (OBT / OBL)** — Shadow Play, Shroud of Absence, Shroud of Night, Blanket of
  Night, Tenebrous Form / Shadow Cast, Shadow Cloak, Where the Veil Thins, Stygian Shroud.
  Stealth plus in-discipline block denial and S:CE backup; Lasombra and Hecata signatures.
  [31 decks OBL stack]
- **PRO stealth** — Earth Control, Form of Mist, Form of the Bat, Earth Meld (+ Rapid Change).
  Stealth that doubles as S:CE — the module that lets Gangrel walls and Stanislava run tight.
  [91 decks Gangrel package]
- **Permanent stealth** — Creepshow Casino, Monastery of Shadows, Elysian Fields, Opium Den,
  Heart of the City, Club Illusion, Toreador Grand Ball. Board stealth that never jams; vote decks
  and repeat-bleeders lean on these + dual-mode cards instead of fat modifier stacks.
- **Block-denial masters** — Pentex™ Subversion, Misdirection, Anarch Troublemaker (+ Mind Numb).
  Paralyze the designated blocker before the lunge; works on votes too.
- **Undirected stealth** — Forgotten Labyrinth (3 stealth, restricted), Perfect Paragon / Force of
  Personality superior (vote-focused, anti-ally). Dual-mode cards cycle when stealth isn't needed:
  tight-but-flexible beats fat-and-jamming.

## Bleed defense

- **AUS bounce trio** — Telepathic Misdirection, My Enemy's Enemy, Eyes of Argus (+ Aura Reading).
  The most played defensive module in the game — bounce and wake in the same slots. [246 decks]
- **DOM bounce** — Deflection (+ Redirection, Murmur of the False Will, Far Mastery for allies'
  sake). Cheap, ubiquitous; on a bleed deck it converts the predator's pressure into prey damage.
- **Baron defense** — Bait and Switch, Organized Resistance (works from a *locked* Baron),
  The Anarch Free Press, Powerbase: Los Angeles. The Anarch answer to DOM/AUS-less crypts; the
  core of what makes Baron toolboxes viable. [30 decks Illegal Brawl kit]
- **Bleed reduction** — Visions of Zapathasura, Party Out of Bounds, Lost in Translation.
  For crypts without bounce disciplines; weaker but stealth-proof.
- **No-bounce substitutes** — Narrow Minds, Archon Investigation (deterrent), Spying Mission
  (avoid feeding the grand-prey on a bounced bleed), Telepathic Counter ⚠. What S&B decks run
  instead of reactions.
- **Bounce counters** — Hide the Mind, Revelations, Approximation of Loyalty, Two Wrongs,
  Contingency Planning, Monocle of Clarity (scout first). Lunge insurance for powerbleed.

## Wakes, intercept & wall kits

- **Generic wakes** — On the Qui Vive, Wake with Evening's Freshness, Forced Awakening. The A&B
  baseline: attack on your turn, still defend after. Their *absence* in a list is the signal
  (all-forward build).
- **AUS wall** — Eyes of Argus, Enhanced Senses, Spirit's Touch, Telepathic Misdirection, Bowl of
  Convergence, Eagle's Sight (cross-table block: protect the grand-prey, hit the grand-predator's
  key action). Intercept, wakes and bounce from the same discipline. [42 decks + Rack/Smiling Jack]
- **ANI intercept** — Raven Spy, Cats' Guidance, Sense the Savage Way, Guard Dogs, Rat's Warning
  (+ Falcon's Eye for cross-table with SPI). Intercept with a combat module attached — no
  equipment needed.
- **Permanent intercept** — KRCG News Radio, Channel 10, WMRH Talk Radio, Wall Street Night, Mr.
  Winthrop, Bowl of Convergence. One intercept is enough vs hunts, equips, recruits and votes;
  these lock that baseline in. [41 decks radio stack]
- **Titled reactions** — Second Tradition: Domain (Camarilla), Organized Resistance (Baron),
  Eternal Vigilance / Under Siege (Sabbat), No Secrets From the Magaji (Laibon). Wake+intercept
  (and for 2nd Trad, +2 intercept) tied to the crypt's titles — the reason "titled crypt" is a
  defensive statement, not just votes. [50 decks 2nd Trad + Parity Shift + Forced March]
- **Protected-permanent payload** — Smiling Jack, The Rack, Powerbase: Montreal / Barranquilla,
  Anarch Revolt, Constant Revolution, Army of Rats, Creeping Sabotage (+ Monkey Wrench lunge, +
  Rewilding as the mirror answer). Fragile masters that pay off only behind real block capacity —
  the wall's actual payload. [26 decks Gangrel Thing kit]
- **Ally wall conversion** — The Unmasking (+ FBI Special Affairs Division to punish ally-killing,
  + Shadow Sentinel/Parijat for wraiths). Turns a recruit swarm into a wall; forces stealth onto
  *votes* as well — the reason vote decks carry anti-ally tech.

## Politics

- **Damage votes core** — Kine Resources Contested, Conservative Agitation (+ Neonate Breach,
  Domain Challenge, Reckless Agitation / Eat the Rich for Anarchs, Camarilla's Iron Fist for
  Camarilla). Diversity is mandatory: it defeats Delaying Tactics, and each different vote is
  another Voter Captivation trigger.
- **Big-cap vote kit** — Reins of Power, Ancient Influence, Political Stranglehold + Banishment
  (removal that also amplifies capacity-scaled votes) + Monastery of Shadows. The Inner
  Circle/Justicar package. [119 decks]
- **Anti-swarm votes** — Anarchist Uprising, Ancilla Empowerment. Dual role: swarm hate AND the
  highest total bead-removal in the game (10+ counters ≈ 2-3 KRC) — they advance the clock even
  with no swarm on the table. [150 decks]
- **Vote push** — Awe, Bewitching Oration, Perfect Paragon, Scalpel Tongue (+ Madrigal, Hall of
  Hades' Court, clan enablers: Ventrue Headquarters, Ominous Chorus, Ferraille). Margin wins:
  every extra vote is also Voter Captivation pool.
- **Voter Captivation loop** — Voter Captivation after every passed referendum; at high counts
  (6-8×) it converts vote margin into the deck's whole defense (see Tap → Voter Cap pump).
- **Titled-crypt toolbox** — all-titled crypts (Princes/Justicars, archbishops, Barons) + Parity
  Shift + Second Tradition / Organized Resistance. Votes, defense and bloat from the crypt itself;
  the political toolbox chassis (Juliet's Dream, Lasombra Pop, Nosferatu Royalty).
- **Vote suppression** — Condemnation: Mute, Arishat, Fabrizia-style minus-votes ⚠, Banishment on
  the key voter. Break the opposing lock before forcing your own.

## Multi-action & A&B engines

- **Unlock modifiers** — Freak Drive, Forced March, Instantaneous Transformation (+ Zephyr, Domain
  of Evernight, CrimethInc.). Act-vote-act turns; needs action *diversity* (NRA rule). Also the
  Delaying Tactics dodge: unlock and call a second, different vote.
- **Unlock riders** — Illegalism [cel], Spectral Servitor's first recruit, Aline Gädeke-style
  abilities, Watchtower: Four Ride Forth, Rutor's Hand, The British Museum. Free A&B stitched onto
  actions the deck takes anyway; cheaper than carrying wakes.
- **Off-turn engines** — Madness Network (+ Reversal of Fortunes: the Madness Reversal combo),
  Enkil Cog. Acting in others' turns is the strongest A&B multiplier in the game; also dodges
  Archon Investigation (out-of-turn) and wins the Edge. Being unlocked is the prerequisite —
  the engine is incomplete without an unlocker (below).
- **Unlockers** — Metro Underground, Mylan Horseed (non-Camarilla only), Homunculus, Eluding the
  Arms of Morpheus. Pure unlock, no action granted: A&B support (act, then still defend) on their
  own, and the enabling piece of off-turn engines — a piece, not the engine.
- **MMPA** — see Bloat; the master-phase equivalent of the above.

## Allies

- **Permanent-intercept allies** — Carlton Van Wyk, Ponticulus, Harzomatuili (+ The Unmasking).
  Self-sufficient blockers with combat ability; Nephandus adds torpor-burning. A table-wide
  deterrent one card at a time. [34 decks Carlton + Nephandus]
- **War Ghoul rush kit** — War Ghoul, Piper, Jake Washington (sacrifice fodder), Fake Out, Trap,
  Ossian, Vozhd. Turn-one rush pressure from the ally side; sacrifice economics beat influence
  economics. J.S. Simmons + Tasha Morgan fit because they are zero-cost: good bleed retainers in
  their own right that *can* be fed to a War Ghoul — useful either way. [22 decks; 28 with
  Simmons + Tasha in Piper lists]
- **Recursive swarms** — Emerald Legionnaire (returns from the ash heap), Spectral Servitor,
  Shambling Hordes + Liquidation/Trochomancy to stock the heap, Charisma for free recruits,
  Khazar's Diary as the payoff variant. Swarm payload that regrows through combat losses —
  pre-nerf Emerald Legion defined the 2022-23 meta (check dates). [20 decks Legionnaire kit]
- **Ally-armed combat swarm** — Underbridge Stray (recruited with Tainted Spring → 3 life / 2
  strength) as the *combatants*, wielding a deliberately disciplineless package (Weighted Walking
  Stick, Target Vitals, Glancing Blow, Lucky Blow, Pulled Fangs — allies play any card with no
  Discipline requirement). Each Stray is triple-duty: a blocker that survives the strike its
  block draws (a successful block leads to combat; +intercept vs stealth via The Unmasking),
  wake battery (burn to unlock a ready minion), and press/combat body. Haven Uncovered
  ×4 marks one target for the whole swarm to descend on in series, beating S:CE/dodge by
  attrition. Fame + Dragonbound convert torpor to pool; Vagabond Mystic heals the swarm; light
  Changeling bleed is the secondary long-game clock. War-Ghoul-adjacent (Tzimisce, Fame/Dragonbound,
  AUS defense) but a distinct engine. [1 deck: TWDA 12149 “Compromise” — see calibration case D]
- **Ally hate & counter-hate** — Entrancement / Xeper, Sultan of Lepers (steal), Scourge of the
  Enochians (vs breed too), FBI SAD (punish), The Uncoiling / Direct Intervention (counter the
  counters). The metagame layer around any ally strategy.

## Combat

- **POT grapple kit** — Immortal Grapple (kills S:CE *and dodges*), Torn Signpost / Increased
  Strength, Thrown Sewer Lid / Roundhouse, Disarm + Taste of Vitae (+ Pulled Fangs to keep the
  victim down without diablerie). The reference close-range kill module; Grapple is what makes the
  rest land. [26 decks Taste + Fame + Disarm]
- **ANI environmental** — Aid from Bats, Carrion Crows (+ Deep Song rush/bleed flex, Terror Frenzy
  / Drawing Out the Beast for range lock, Canine Horde / Voracious Vermin anti-weapon). 3 damage
  with press at no blood, undodgeable; pairs with POT (rush) or intercept (wall). [41 decks]
- **CEL guns** — Concealed Weapon + .44 Magnum / Sniper Rifle, Blur, Pursuit, Psyche!, Sideslip
  (+ Disguised Weapon, Dragonbound/Fame payload). Long-range strikes plus additional strikes;
  Psyche! doubles as anti-S:CE. [27-31 decks variants]
- **Marduk theft-at-range** (CEL+THA, Banu Haqim) — Hunger of Marduk (played before range: this
  round, strikes become ranged blood steal) + maneuvers to long (Blur, Pursuit, Sideslip) +
  additional strikes (Quickness, Blur) so one round = several steals; Rego Motum as cover. Steal
  is not damage — prevention doesn't apply — and the blood loops back to the striker: grinding
  from a range where most modules can't answer. The modern Haqim Royalty combat identity.
  [28 decks Haqim kit; 17 Blur + Fast Hands]
- **Gargoyle VIS+POT kit** (Tupdogs) — Brick by Brick (set the range at superior, or 2R strike
  with maneuver), Raking Talons (this Gargoyle's hand strikes aggravated for the whole combat),
  Immortal Grapple, Torn Signpost, Target Vitals (+ Rock Cat as the ally companion). Range
  control into aggravated close-range beatdown, from disposable weenie rushers whose trade is
  always favorable. Brick by Brick + Raking Talons is the strongest co-occurrence pair in the
  whole TWDA mining run (lift 59). [25 decks with Immortal Grapple + Graverobbing]
- **FOR grinder** — Weighted Walking Stick, Rolling with the Punches, Hidden Strength,
  Indomitability, Soak. Prevent-and-outlast: win the blood exchange, not the round. [21 decks]
- **THA blood-theft** — Theft of Vitae, Rego Motum (+ Walk of Flame). A 4-blood unpreventable
  swing per round: grinding as economy, the blood loops back. [21 decks Tremere kit]
- **VIC aggravated** — Chiropteran Marauder, Breath of the Dragon (+ Horrid Form, Inner Essence).
  Agg at any range with prevention attached; burns torpored vampires outright. [29+ decks]
- **S:CE suites** — PRE (Majesty, Staredown, Force of Personality), PRO (Earth Meld, Form of
  Mist), OBT (Oubliette, Shadow Body), big-cap DOM/OBF (Obedience, Mental Maze). The defensive
  posture: pure avoidance, cycles on actions in forward decks.
- **Anti-S:CE** — Immortal Grapple, Psyche!, Telepathic Tracking, NSA Trio (blockers only),
  Relentless Reaper. What separates a real rush threat from a nuisance.
- **Range control** — maneuvers everywhere (Dust Up, Bollix, Blur…) + set-range (Sniper Rifle,
  Shadow Step, High Ground). Whoever fixes the range chooses which module functions; the answer to
  both gun decks and Marduk-style blood theft.
- **Combat → pool conversion** — Fame, Tension in the Ranks, Dragonbound (+ Anarch Revolt).
  The cards that make combat a payload at all; without them rushing is pure table control.
- **Disciplineless combat = possible ally armament** — a package with no Discipline requirement
  (Weighted Walking Stick, Target Vitals, Glancing Blow, Lucky Blow, Pulled Fangs) may be built so
  *allies* wield it; in an ally-swarm deck the allies are the fighters, not the crypt. See Allies
  → Ally-armed combat swarm.

## Counters & tech

- **Anti-vote** — Delaying Tactics (forces vote diversity table-wide), Poison Pill (cheaper,
  vote-deck-borne), Fear of Mekhet (vs Inner Circle crypts), Yawp Court ⚠.
- **Master denial** — Sudden Reversal, Wash, Bleeding the Vine. Countering a bloat master IS a
  bleed (3-5 pool swing); also answers Pentex, permanent intercept, and Villein economies.
- **Action/card cancel** — Direct Intervention (the classic; cancelling a modifier bars its replay
  that action), Dark Influences (denies the card all turn — often better), Bollix [pre] (cancels
  grapples in the mirror).
- **Breed/swarm hate** — Scourge of the Enochians (+ The Uncoiling as the counter-counter).
- **Greed punish** — Archon Investigation: the reason 3 is "the Archon limit"; carrying one *is*
  bleed defense.
- **Contest as a weapon** — Secure Haven (protect the star / contest the opponent's), Garibaldi
  contested to deny its S:CE, Aranthebes contest tech. Uniques are a battlefield.
- **Timing gifts** ⚠ — Millicent Smith passed to the predator (any blocked minion burns): cards
  donated cross-table as deterrence.

## Star engines (signature combos, one-liners)

- **Enkil Cog + Instantaneous Transformation + Homunculus** — The Unnamed: bleed on every player's
  turn. [103 decks contain the pair-core]
- **Madness Network + Reversal of Fortunes** — Madness Reversal: play twice as much as the table.
- **Montano + Baltimore Purge + Graverobbing + Heidelberg Castle** — steal via torpor, strip blood.
- **Mind Rape + Heidelberg / + Madness Network** — steal, strip, act with it immediately.
- **Cybele + Call the Great Beast + Soul Gem of Etrius (+ Magic of the Smith, Truth of a Thousand
  Lies)** — the Cybelotron resurrection loop.
- **Nergal + Call the Great Beast + Soul Gem** — summon a loaded Beast, refresh Nergal.
- **Shalmath + Summon History (+ Ankara Citadel first)** — a fetch engine as a crypt.
- **Rachel Brandywine + Homunculus + Madness Network** — bleed across consecutive turns.
- **Week of Nightmares + Dabbler + Break the Bonds kit** — the Ravnos Bonds explosion. [29 decks]
- **Khazar's Diary + expendable allies (Jake, Cry Wolf, Shambling Hordes)** — every dead ally is a
  Diary counter.
- **Hesha Ruhadze + equipment fetchers (Angel Chavarria, Hag's Wrinkles, Learjet) + Nod** — the
  20+ bleed Emporium.
- **Lutz von Hohenzollern + vote diversity** — every passed referendum pings the prey: utility
  votes become payload.
- **Powerbase: Savannah + Salt of Thoth** — S:CE denial as a board state; jams wall hands.
