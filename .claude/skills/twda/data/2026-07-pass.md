q = qualifying wins: tournaments with **20+ players** in the **rolling 3-year
window** (currently since 2023-07-05). With L = best score below any anomaly:

1. **Anomaly** 💀 (skull/trophy icon TBD): leader > 2×L → sits above the
   ladder. Currently fires: Gangrel Resistance 44 > 2×18.
2. **Top tier**: ⌈L/2⌉ … L → 9–18q.
3. **Runner-ups**: next halving → 4–8q.
4. **New kids**: 2–3q AND the archetype is new (first competitive appearance
   within the window; owner adjudicates ambiguous ages).
5. **Old guard** (NEW subsection): 3q, old archetype — floor at 3 to keep it
   quiet. Owner adjudications this pass: Matasuntha and Nergal Beast are old
   (the 5y window under-measures archetype age).
6. Everything else: archive (existing pages) or no page.
