Baali and their unique discipline {{ dai }} provide good clan cards. Their vampires are all Infernal, and 1 pool per turn is a high cost, but some of them, combined with the clan cards, have abilities matching this cost. There are multiple archetypes used by the Baali clan:

  • {{ the_dark_side_of_politics }} archetype, using {{ giotto_verducci }} and {{ kaymakli_fragment }} to get titled vampires into play fast and cheap.
  • The {{ girls_will_find_inner_circles }} archetype, using {{ cybele }} to get more Master actions.
  • The {{ the_unnamed_arch }} archetype, a stealth & bleed construct starring, obviously, {{ the_unnamed }}.
  • The {{ council_of_doom }} archetype, starring {{ huitzilopochtli }} along some Inner Circle vampires.

The titled vampires of the clan, {{ arishat }}, {{ xeper }} and {{ annazir }}, also offer good options for political builds.
