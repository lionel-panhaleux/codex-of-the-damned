Highlights

This is a breed and boon deck starring {{ anson }} and uses {{ pre }} as the discipline to push votes. The purpose is to have him out as fast as possible to use {{ politics }} to kill your prey.

Tips & Tricks

Using {{ grooming }} cost quite an amount of blood and it is necessary to replenish your vampire from time to time. Use {{ voter_cap }} to do this instead of hunting as much as you can. This way you won't lose any action and it will be easier to maximise the damages you can deal to your prey.

{{ krc }} is the best action to deal massive damages. It is possible to negiciate with your great predator how to assign the damages. Do not forget that you still have to kill your prey and dealing too much damages to your predator will only help your great predator do their point and come after you more quickly.

{{ parity_shift }} can only be called by Princes in this deck : {{ anson }} and {{ tatiana }}. Be sure to always have one of them available to call for {{ parity_shift }} as it is the only way to {{ bloat }} without {{ grooming }}. {{ toreador_justicar }} is the only way to have more vampires able to call for a {{ parity_shift }}.

It is a good idea when the grand-prey deck has good block capacity: they do not get the chance to block the undirected vote actions until they are either the prey or the predator. {{ tgb }} is of great help against this kind of deck as a low capacity vampire can be chosen to make all votes from {{ anson }} unblockable.

Suggested changes

This deck contains cards that are banned ({{ anthelios }} and {{ temptation }}) and cards that have been errata'ed ({{ pentex_subversion }} and {{ parity_shift }}). As detailed hereunder, {{ parity_shift }} is still good but the other cards need to be replaced.

My suggestions are one {{ elder_library }} and one extra {{krc}}. The {{elder_library }} will help you have more cards in hand. The purpose is to have a bigger and less jam-able hand. This Master card is not used often and it will help you not to contest it to keep your pool to swarm.
