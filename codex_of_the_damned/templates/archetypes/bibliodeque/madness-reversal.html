Highlights

This is a special deck using {{ madness_network }} and {{ reversal_of_fortunes }}.

The idea is to get some low capacity Malkavians out fast together with a {{ madness_network }}, then play {{ reversal_of_fortunes }} during the prey's turn to play again, then again on the predator's turn to play again, etc. playing twice as much as the opponents.

The first vampires get a bit locked in the combination, and the rest of the deck focuses on playing a lot of Master cards, {{ brainwash }} and {{ kaymakli_nightmares }} to prevent the opponents of even getting a vampire out.

The deck then kills with simple bleeds and {{ smiling_jack_the_anarch }}.

Tips & Tricks

Rotation is tricky in this deck, and {{ dreams_of_the_sphinx }}, {{ specialization }} and {{ heart_of_nizchetus }} all help in this regard.

Although {{ victor_pelletier }} is not a Malkavian, his ability is priceless in this deck, as {{ reversal_of_fortunes }} needs some votes to get through.

Variants

There are not many variants of this deck. {{ orian_gissler_s_deck }} from 2009 was the original attempt.
