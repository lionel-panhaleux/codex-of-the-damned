Highlights

This is a leeching deck using {{ VAL }} to disrupt the bleeds of one's prey. The objective is to reduce the pool of one's prey to 0 using {{ operation }} and preventing it to gain the edge using {{ hidetheheart }}.

The deck uses low to mid capacity vampires get the two cards above running as quickly as possible. The rest of the deck is protection against situations that may arise when trying to deal with the prey.

Tips & Tricks

Try to get an Osebo vampire first to start putting some {{ operation }} into play and then aim to get a {{ VAL }} vampire and starting to disrupt your prey's bleeds.

As the game progresses, you might have more than five {{ operation }} in play and you'll be nearing killing your prey but be sure that your great-prey does not have the Edge the moment you'll be doing your point because they will be able to get some of your prey's pool by burning the Edge.
