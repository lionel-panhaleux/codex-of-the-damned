Top Tier

Here is a list of the best Vampire: the Eternal Struggle deck archetypes. Every archetype is presented with {{ twda }} deck list examples, main variations, and a play guide.

Only archetypes having proven themselves against at least 20 players twice in the last 5 years are taken into account, and only if one of these wins is a Continental Championship or if they have at least a third win. Example decklists are the most recent ones, or the ones with a Continental Championship win. Decks relying heavily on mechanics that have since been errated are not included.

Please note that this list is built purely on TWDA statistics: more than a third of tournaments wins are achieved with unrelated decks.

Archive

For your enjoyment, you'll find here a collection of archetypes that did not make it to the top tier lists, but are still very much viable.

Quite a few of these lists come from the « Bibliodèque »: a French project made of more than a hundred decklists based on Tournament winning decklists or archetypes to help people build decks for their casual play or tournaments, and ultimately print those decks in proxy to have a library of decks available for their playgroup.

Many thanks to the French people helping in various ways: TTC_master acting as lead designer, Ezra, Tim, Cordovader, Darkal, Stone and so on... and to the players whose decks have served as a basis for the decks presented here (or were shamelessly copied directly).

You may find the complete historic of the project in the {{ french_vekn_forum }} and the final Bibliodèque with proxies on {{ google_drive }}.
