Top Tier

Here is a list of the best Vampire: the Eternal Struggle deck archetypes. Every archetype is presented with {{ twda }} deck list examples, main variations, and a play guide.

Archetypes are tiered by their number of qualifying wins: victories in tournaments with 20 players or more in the last 3 years. Top tier archetypes score at least half as many qualifying wins as the current leader. Example decklists are recent qualifying wins, Continental or National championships when possible. Decks relying heavily on mechanics that have since been errated are not included.

The 💀 icon singles out a dominating archetype: more than twice the qualifying wins of any other. It is not just the top of the list, it is the deck to beat.

Please note that this list is built purely on TWDA statistics: more than a third of tournaments wins are achieved with unrelated decks.
